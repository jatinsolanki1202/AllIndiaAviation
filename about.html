AIAA at a Glance

Established Since 2005, a excellence in the field of training aspirants for Aviation & Hospitality (Airline, Travel, Tourism and Hotels). Institute runs under the aegis of Buddha Foundation registered under the Mumbai Public Trust Act.29,1950 with registration no. E/3215/Anand, approved by Government of Gujarat. An ISO 9001:2015 certified Institute and. Courses designed to suit the industry's requirements. Job training with leading Airline, Travel Industries, Star hotels & Resorts. Campus recruitments conducted at AIAA by Airline companies and Hospitality Industries.
Approved as a Study center for Aviation related Management program conducted by Bharat Sevak Samaj - an Agency promoted by Govt. of India

AIMS & OBJETIVES

- The 'Buddha Foundation' has come into existence for the upbringing of Aeronautical Education which has vast scopes in India as well as abroad.

- As we are having the official authority granted by the Govt. of India under the Trust Act. 29,1950, to run technical,Industrial, Commercial, Vocational or any kind of academic educational Institute.

- Our basic aim is to provide an opportunity to those who seek to make an Aviation / Hospitality field as their professions.

- The society is established to provide the best training especially in the field of Aeronautics and Hospitality Industries.

- Our main objective is to put in valuable efforts to each and every irrespective of cast, creed, sex and religion.

- To import training for the welfare and bright future of Indian Society.

Director’s desk

“ALL INDIA AVIATION ACADEMY” feels proud to warmly welcome you to one of the most renowned and esteemed educational organizations where professionals in the aviation field like pilots, air-hostesses and flight pursers are successfully trained. Road of progress takes one successfully from what he/she is to what he/she is capable of. Our institute with an amalgamation of tradition and modernity envisions holistic development of students to keep in step with the progressive st strides being made in the 21 century. We firmly believe in the adage 'The sky is the limit' and do our best to orient our students to achieve that ultimate objective. We also inculcate values of discipline, character building and team work in our students. Various facilities provided at our institute coupled with the individual attention paid to each student help bring about multidimensional personality growth and put students on the path of success. We train our students in a manner that no other institute does for, we may not do big things but we do small things with great care and commitment. We boast of one of the best faculties in terms of experience, calibre and competence. All our faculties had long association in the fields of aviation and hospitality. We continuously strive for excellence not only in imparting high class education but also in upgrading our facilities with the help of creating effective resources and environment. In order to stand apart among the horde of institutes teaching the same curricula, AIAA relentlessly tries to keep pace with ever changing demands of the industry. Our campus is located at a serene and noise free zone of the city affording conducive environment for undisturbed learning. Students passing out from the portals of our institute are able to create a niche for themselves which others find hard to match. In the institute our endeavour is to bestow students with finer qualities such as morality, truthfulness, patriotism and spirit of healthy competition. Nurturing outstanding professional skills and character building traits go along way in lifting students' composite personality to a much higher plane. All India Aviation Academy distinguishes itself in providing students value for their money in terms of imparting top of the drawer training in various professional and vocational courses. Over the past couple of years it has created a niche for itself for excellent faculty, campus, training aids and placement assistance. Its learned, dedicated and committed faculty probably is one of the best in the region. In addition to thoroughly teaching the professional subjects, institute spares no efforts in holistically building the personality of the students. While making no false promises to lure the students, AIAA goes all out to assist its students in achieving gainful employment. A number of ex-students of the institutes who are progressing in their chosen careers bear testimony to the fact. In recent months, institute held two job fairs in the campus where leading placement companies of north India were invited. In these fairs candidates from outside AIAA were also given access to present themselves before the interview board. The commercial pilot course of the institute is quite popular and has generated and immense interest among the youth from even distant areas. Currently many students of the institute are undergoing flying training in World Flying Clubs. After having successfully run the aviation and Hospitality courses for over ten years, AIAA has now diversified its operation in other academic and professional training programmes. Considering its excellent track record, This is a major step towards providing quality education to the discerning students.
